  • Steps to cancel your Netflix subscription
    1. Visit Netflix.com on any web browser and sign in to your account.
    2. Tap three-dash menu in the top left side.
    3. Now head to Account.
    4. Scroll down and click on Cancel Membership available under Membership & Billing.
    5. Once done, you will be notified that your cancellation will be effective at the end of your current billing period.
    6. Tap the blue Finish Cancellation button to confirm the cancellation of your Netflix subscription and it's done.
